why Choose Us?

Welcome to SBS restaurant, where every dish tells a story and every meal is a journey through time and taste. Our restaurant's foundation is built upon a rich tapestry of tradition, passion, and a deep-rooted love for culinary craftsmanship. Our story begins decades ago, in a small village nestled amidst rolling hills and fertile valleys. It was here that our founder, Aaditya, first discovered his love for cooking in his grandmother's kitchen. From her, he learned the art of transforming simple ingredients into culinary masterpieces, infused with love and tradition. With each dish she prepared,Aaditya's grandmother shared tales of family lore and cherished recipes passed down through generations. These culinary traditions became the foundation of SBS restaurant, a place where heritage and innovation merge to create unforgettable dining experiences. Driven by a desire to share his passion for food with the world, Aaditya embarked on a journey of culinary exploration, traveling far and wide to study under renowned chefs and immerse himself in diverse culinary traditions. Along the way, he honed his skills and developed a unique culinary philosophy that would come to define SBS restaurant. Upon returning to his hometown, Aaditya realized his dream of opening a restaurant that would pay homage to the flavors of his childhood while embracing the excitement of new culinary horizons. And so, SBS restaurant was born.
